Frequently Asked Questions

Segmentation Model

What modifications were made to create the i-UNet?
The i-UNet integrates attention mechanisms and a ResNet50 backbone to improve feature extraction and segmentation precision, reducing overfitting and enhancing boundary detail.
How does attention enhance global feature capture?
Attention mechanisms allow the network to weigh critical regions more effectively, leading to higher Dice scores and reduced loss.
